Welcome to Organized Closet Domain -- your all-in-one virtual closet organizer application.

Click on the "Closet!" link in the navigation bar above to see the inventory of all the items in your closet, where you can add, edit or delete individual items. You can also add items to outfits you create in the "Outfits!" section.

Click on the "Outfits!" link in the navigation bar above to create, view, and modify outfits.

Features Coming Soon

  • The ability to add outfits to days on a calendar that will also contain updated weather information for a specified zipcode.
  • Suitcases! where you can add outfits or individual items to a suitcase for any upcoming trip. Enter in your destination and updated weather information will also be available.
  • Social Closet! where you can share outfit ideas with your friends!
